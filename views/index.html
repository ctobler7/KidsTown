The Town

Kids Town is a child sized town filled with pretend play activities!

The House

Cook up some breakfast in the kitchen, rock a baby doll to sleep in the nursery, or work in the garden in the front yard!

The School

Be a teacher, be a student, write on the chalkboard, or solve a puzzle!

The Pizzeria

May I take your order? Build your customer’s pizza and then place it in the brick oven to cook!

The Post Office

Sort the incoming letters, stamp the postcards, and deliver the mail to the rest of Kids Town!

The Hamburger Shop

Work the drive thru, cook the burgers, fry the French fries, and bag it up to go!

The Flower Shop

Water the flowers and make beautiful bouquets!

The Toy Shoppe

Ball run, spinning discs, magnetic paper dolls! Explore the unique toys in this Toy Shoppe!

The Beauty Salon

Time for a trim? Sit in the spinning salon chairs! Don’t forget your manicure!

The Market

Get your groceries here! Push a grocery cart and load it up before checking out!

The Animal Clinic

After a well check, pamper your pet with a bath and a treat!

The Sweet Shoppe

Candy, cupcakes, cookies and more! Bake all the yummy treats and put them on display!

The Garage

Work under the hood, change the tires, fill up the gas, and get a car wash!

To sweeten your stay with us, we offer specialty sodas and treats for purchase. Click here for our menu.
